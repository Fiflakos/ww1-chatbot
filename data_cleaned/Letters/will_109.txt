Author: F M ODonnell Date: 14 November 1917 Nationality: Australian Text: 3rd Australian DivisionBase Depot.14.11.1917.General Sir W.R. BirdwoodKCB. K.C.S.I. KCMG. CIE. DSO.Dear General Birdwood,Would youplease accept my hearty congratulationson your promotion to rank ofGeneral which has appeared in thepress today. May you be longspared to continue the good workand after triumphing over the enemyto come to our dear Australia toremain one of us until the end of thechapter.Yours truelyF.M. O'Donnell

Author: Walter H Long Date: 14 March 1917 Location: Anzac Nationality: British Text: COLONIAL OFFICEMy dear General.I must send you aline of warm thanksfor your personal kindness& hospitality. I wasvery greatly interested in,& impressed by, all I saw& came away fullyconvinced that ourDominion troops arewell able to continueto take the same brilliant share ofthis fresh struggle asthey have done fromfor beginning.I enjoyed my ride onthat charming horsemore than I can say &was much struck by thethoroughness of histraining & his fineappearance of the waler My very warm thanks.Sincerely yoursWalter H Long14. 3. 1917.

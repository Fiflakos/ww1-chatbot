Author: Harold Edwin Salisbury Rank: Captain Date: 24 March 1916 Nationality: Australian Text: mon tholss Lient Iighs Chourt rpent fine chap sn y three Comepte Copy Horfer Camphell Hosper of Nenw. crctil Major or Atlot hovnge D.C.O Rop Reart an Drear MA tc and Maunie Guor. I had a long you with latter out in desert Towler & myalf in Euey Canal to. Regative are in my trunk. No news at pusent. Segt Ine my lemp captenney swill be confireed if I have the luch to keep O.K. Anyhow at precent hold rank &hraw pay Your loving son, Rollo

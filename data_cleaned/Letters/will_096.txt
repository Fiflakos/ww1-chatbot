Author: W H Greenly Date: 30 July 1917 Location: Sussex Nationality: British Text: 30.7.1917My dear General -The very best ofthanks for yourcharming letter.Of course I am absolutelydelighted & the proudestman in all theworld : I can only say it was exceedinglyfortunate for me thatyou were not eligible!in spite of technicalitieswe always regard youas a 12th Lancer & areall very fond of theconnections.I'll tell Fane how sorryyou were first to havemissed seeing thembut I hope post-opsyou'll be able to makeit good soon. Sofar as we can tellwe may be aboutthis area some time& should love to see you whenever you wouldset a day to comeover.ForeverWH Greenly

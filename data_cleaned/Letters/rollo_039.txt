Author: Harold Edwin Salisbury Location: Anzac Cove Date: 5 August 1915 Nationality: Australian Text: 1st LieutI wrote earlier but did not dare send it in viewof circumstances - but look up the papers of this dateet sequ. Just recd word that Hancock & Ihave recd. our second stars - so are now fullblown Lieutenants.I hope to write next week from elsewhere -but this must suffice at present.Good luck - best love. Rollo.

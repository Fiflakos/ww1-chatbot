Author: Toms Mother Date: 24 February 1915 Location: Cortoon Nationality: Australian Text: Cortoon24th February, 1915.My dear old boyJust a word or twofor as usual mail night & several towrite. I wonder what you will thinkof Leo enlisting I wish you were together.I cannot realize he has goneinto camp. Linda feels keenly Leo'sgoing but she & Eileen might comforteach other and we must be braveand not shirk our duty helping eachother all we can during this terriblewar. We are taking care of your goodsalso Leo's & Georges we have your bookcase & chairs in the little front roomyour chest drawers in Jacks. Oh howWe will yearn for you & Leo to come home.Now dear goodnight God bless youMother

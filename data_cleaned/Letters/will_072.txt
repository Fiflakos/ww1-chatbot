Author: Walter H Long Date: 13 March 1917 Location: Zepp Nationality: British Text: COLONIAL OFFICE 17.3. 1917My dear GeneralMy most hearty congratnsupon your being first intoBapaume. A greatfeat & one on whichthe Australian troopsare to be heartilycongratulated. I feltsure they meant toget there very soon.What an extraordinaryupheaval in Russia!So far as one can judgefrom Buchanan's telegramsthe change is all forthe good. This new Gov. mean to persecutethe War with renewedvigour so great goodought to result.An abortive Zeppraid here & one of themdowned by the French.Sincerl yoursWalter H. Long

Author: Harold Edwin Salisbury Date: 7 September 1915 Nationality: Australian Text: We laid our wire ok but Cox stopped a bullet 9.15right through his stomach - but tho in great pain hetook it like a Briton - he was one of my old platoon.Nos. 9 & 10 have done most of the wire laying so farAt present I am acting OC of C Company - but Iwill be out again tonight to finish the wire -as Idon't like asking the men to do a risky job withoutdoing my share too. I read my mail. The weirdcontraption Wrumps sent as a head dress - I willwear it tho it gives no protection to my facial adornment.Lots of correspondence to answer so will close. Sox are ok. Rollo

Author: Sidney Broad Hodges. Date: 30December 1915. Location: Longmoor, England. Regiment:Royal Engineers Railway Troop. Regiment number:138032. Rank:Sapper. Nationality: British. I might say we are all merry and bright and not down hearted yet. Dear Mr Hunt, Received card alright for which I must thank you very much. We are still at Longmoor and no signs of moving as I have been made cooks mate and had to work very hard all through Xmas. Sapper Hodges Front of postcard: I am sending you this THE FIRST OR GRENADIER REGIMENT OF FOOT GUARDS ToWish youAPleasant Christmas

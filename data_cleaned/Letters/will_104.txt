Author: C M Wagstaff Date: 13 November 1917 Nationality: Australian Text: 13th November 1917.My dear GeneralMay I offer you myvery best congratulations onyour new promotion and therecent high honour - I didnot know of either when Isaw you the other day - &did not say anything aboutthem.You know how we all feelthat these things have beenvery late in coming to you and how delighted everybodyis that at last you aregetting things you havedeserved for so long.I hope you are going.to take a little time offnow - you have had avery strenuous time, andyou need a short rest atany rate. My wife sends her bestcongratulations to LadyBirdwood -Yours sincerely -C.M.Wagstaff.

Author: Stephen Midgley Date: 23 November 1917 Location: London Nationality: Australian Text: REGENT PALACE HOTEL,PICCADILLY CIRCUS, LONDON, W.1.23rd November 1917Dear General.Just a line to say goodbyeI expect to leave for Australia ina few days time. I trust itsneedless for me to say how verysorry I was to be no longer ableto serve under you. I havefinished well in that I am ableto say I Terminated my serviceunder the most distinguishedand brave General of this age.I was so glad a few daysago to see you had been elevatedto the rank of full General, andI heartily congratulate youMy wife will remain inEngland until such time Iknow what shape my futureis to take Yours Sincerely.Stephen Midgley.

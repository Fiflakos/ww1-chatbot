Author: Harold Edwin Salisbury Location: Habeita CampDate: 10 February 1916 Nationality: Australian Text: Just a very brief note as mail leaves camp-we are still all awaiting Jacko if his travelling tous - hes got fine weather for it - but youllwill know whats doing before this reaches you.Plenty of work - digging - outposts nightly -but everyones very fit - & very eager to hearthe order 3 rounds Rapid etcNo news otherwise - desert in this part is abit changed - marvellous what MacLagans Circusdoes when it when it stays in such places as Habeita.Will write a pukka letter later on Sunday- so closewith much love- and hope youre all as well as I am.Your loving Rollo

Author: Wilfred Evans Date: 27 January 1919 Regiment: 9th Light Horse Location: Blue Funnel Line S S Aeneas Nationality: Australian Text: CERTUM PETE FINEMTHE BLUE FUNNEL LINES.S. "AENEAS."27.1.1919My dear mother,Just ahurried scrawl to saythat we are just goingin to Albany & shouldarrive in Sydney inabout a weeks time -watch the papersAll well & have hada good trip - don'tget too excited now.I am not going totry & tell you aboutthe voyage as Iexpect to be with youabout a day or soafter this letter reachesyou so will closewith much lovefromYour loving sonWilf.

This well-known hotel has been refurnished and thoroughly renewed in everyrespect, so that is now possesses all the requirements and elegance of afirstclass house, and no one residing there will miss his home-comforts.As it is situated in the centre of the city, opposite the beautiful EsbekiehGarden, and close to the Opera-house, the banks, post and telegraph offices, Cook'soffice, etc., it is specially adapted for families. Sitting in the comfortable verandah,visitors can watch the life and traffic of Cairo better than from any other hotel.There are more than 150 rooms and saloons, bar, ladies' parlour, and a hallwhich has been transformed into a winter-garden. Almost all the rooms looksouthward, and have balconies open to the sunshine all day long, offering to visitorsfrom the cold north of Europe the most agreeable and salubrious sojourn.

Author: Charles Rosenthal Date: 14 October 1917 Nationality: British Text: October 14th 1917.Dear Sir William.May I on behalf of myselfand the 9th Aus Infantry Brigadetender our very sincere congratulationson your recent appointment as A.D.CGeneral to His Majesty, and yourpromotion to General rank.Every additional honour you receiveis a source of the liveliest satisfactionto us all - but at the same timewe hope your new rank will notmean severance from the Australian Forces. We are now settled in our new Sector(old sector to many of us) and wefind the conditions concerning accommodationfor front line troops is quite good.Yours Sincerely,Charles Rosenthal

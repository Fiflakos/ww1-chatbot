Author: Harold Edwin Salisbury Location: SerapeumDate: 7 May 1916 Nationality: Australian Text:Don't be surprised or anxious if my letters are more irregular then usual - as transport islimited & if we go inland - mails will be few & far between - & also will be held up.I will write frequently, & chance the mails beingblocked. Best of all good wishes - health - goodluck & very much love to you all - Much loveRollo

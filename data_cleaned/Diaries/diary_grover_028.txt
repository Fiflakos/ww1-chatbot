Author: Grover Carter Date: 10 April 1918 Rank: Lieutenant Nationality: American Text: April 10, l918 Reported to concentration camp. Many civies but none sick. Later was called to RR station. Found a woman in labor. Could find no room for her in houses. So put her on bell tent1 bags and delivered her before many onlookers. Did not have time to wait for soap and water before delivery. All done under very septic conditions. Blanche Francois, baby boy, born 2:15 p.m. Very little haemorrhage, placenta delivered 2:30 intact.

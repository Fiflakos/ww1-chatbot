Author: Grover Carter Date: 25 April 1918 Rank: Lieutenant Nationality: American Text: April 25, 1918 Left at 9:30 for Hamoeke [probably meant Helhoek, Belgium] Arrived about 11:15 a.m. At 12:30 had orders to move at once as Bosche had captured Kemmel. Left at 3 p.m. for battery position near Reningelst.

Author: Thomas James Richards. Date: 26th September 1914. Nationality: Australian. Saturday 26thI went on picket thismorning at 3.30 until 6.I rather enjoyed it, regardless ofthe cold, and the stars werevery entertaining and looked sopale and helpless at as the Sunrose and out shone. themWe have our own kits packed ^delivered to be forwarded butfind the Colonne has ordersfrom headquarters to stand byand not go aboard to-day This is extremely disappointingas we have prepared for leavingto-day or early to-morrowmorning. I missed myappointment with W L.B badly to-night,and went back to campat 8 p.m. feeling annoyed.

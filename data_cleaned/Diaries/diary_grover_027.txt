Author: Grover Carter Date: 9 April 1918 Rank: Lieutenant Nationality: American Text: April 9, 1918 School dismissed at noon. Ordered to Strozule [Strazeele] to attend French civilians who were evacuating. No train from Steenwerck. Met a Canadian, Capt. Kendall, who had charge of RR. Came to Strozule c[with] two orderlies in his car. Spent night in his hut.

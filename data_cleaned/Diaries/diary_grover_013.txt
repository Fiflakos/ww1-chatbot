Author: Grover Carter Date: 24 March 1918 Rank: Lieutenant Nationality: American Text: March 24, 1918 Still feeling bum. Reported to Railway Transport Officer at 9 a.m. To report at 10:30. Dont seem able to get away as railroad lines are congested. Trains bringing in wounded. Left Boulogne at 1 p.m. and reached Candas at 6:30 p.m. Changed trains. Left at 10 p.m. and arrived Doullens at 4 a.m. Were on siding for about 3 hrs. Like to have frozen. Slept in station. No hotels available.

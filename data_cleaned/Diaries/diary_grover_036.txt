Author: Grover Carter Date: 18 April 1918 Rank: Lieutenant Nationality: American Text: April 18, 1918 Dressed cases all morning. Sent to R.A.P. [Regimental Aid Post] near Locre [Loker] Saw many dead men & horses on the road. The Hun had shelled it very heavily. Later was sent to occupy A.D.S. near Mont Noir. Slept in the cellar. Snowed all night. Met 2 U.S.A. lieutenants, Crouse & Hule.

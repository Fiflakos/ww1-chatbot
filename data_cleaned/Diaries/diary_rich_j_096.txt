Author: Thomas James Richards. Date: 30th September 1914. Nationality: Australian. Wednesday Sept. 30thGeneral leave 5 to 10pmOur new camp contains 9 menthe ground is very uneven andmakes one very stiff to sleep on.The three buglers are in ourtent and everything seems veryreasonableA good long walk, carrying stretchersthrough the pretty Spring folagfoliaged Centennial Park wasvery pleasing. The lake ofwater lilys were delightful.I went to Manly at nightand saw W.B. This girldoes attract me, and sometimesevens haunts me, yet when Iattempt to reason matters out Icannot see matrimony loomingat all. Circumstances of canceprevents me considering the pointat present.Camp at 12 o'clock

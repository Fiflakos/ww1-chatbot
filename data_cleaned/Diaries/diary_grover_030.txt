Author: Grover Carter Date: 12 April 1918 Rank: Lieutenant Nationality: American Text: April 12, 1918 Reported R[ailway] T[raffic] O[fficer] at 9 a.m. Could not tell me where my unit was. Waited for 34th Div. train (supply). Took a lorrie at 2 p.m. and went to Lynde. Had had nothing to eat since night before. Felt all in. Had severe headaches and temperature 102.6c

Author: Thomas James Richards. Date: 25th October 1914. Nationality: Australian. Mail Day Sunday Oct. 25th It was on the 26th Aug. that Fritz Schwarz and I came into camp. This is over two months ago. Letters to Sponza, Mother, Bill & Bert Boardman, F french, Elvy, Broad, Church passed off pleasantly to day. We are expected to arrive at Albany to-morrow but have no idea as to whether we will be allowed ashore or not. All hands are busy writing yet there are a number of gambling schools going strong, many games have been barred such as two up, crown & anchor, under & over 7. One man got 90 hours in the cells for playing crown & anchor. Several new cases of pox in the hospital to day.

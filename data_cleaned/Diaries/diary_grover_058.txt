Author: Grover Carter Date: 11 May 1918 Rank: Lieutenant Nationality: American Text: May 11, 1918 Had a French major to lunch with us today. He has been working with U.S.A. boys and speaks very highly of them. Made me feel good to my toes. Letter from home dated Mar. 31st. Had a few gas shells put near us today but was not very strong. Some shelling around us in p.m.

Author: Grover Carter Date: 5 April 1918 Rank: Lieutenant Nationality: American Text: April 5, 1918 Left at 8:30 a.m. Went to Pont Nieppe and picked up a U.S.A. boy and a R.A.M.C. boy. Then proceeded to the trenches. Walked down Lunatic Lane and other trenches nearby Armentieres. Back for lunch. Very muddy and slippery.

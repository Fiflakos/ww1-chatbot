Author: Grover Carter Date: 3 April 1918 Rank: Lieutenant Nationality: American Text: April 3, 1918 Rained all night and still at it this a.m. Orders to return to my unit today. Very pleased, however is more safe here. Very cold, damp & gloomy. Letters from Ross [brother], Louise, Howard Houston & Mena Scott of Ann Arbor. Reached my unit about 5:30 p.m. Had letters from Mother & Louise. Nice Armstrong Hut to share with Lt. Hughes, R.A.M.C., a newcomer.

Author: Grover Carter Date: 1 April 1918 Rank: Lieutenant Nationality: American Text: April 1, 1918 Was assigned a ward today. Have had 7 or 8 cases. All to be evacuated soon. Very nice day but a little chilly. Have a slightly sore throat. Sleeping in a tent but have a bed and mattress. Still have a touch of the blues. About 60 more patients admitted.

Author: Frederick Harold Tubb Date: Friday 04.06.1915. Nationality: Australian. Issued clothing to themen. Went in charge of the horses of ourcamp to swimming parade Capt Robincame too we had a grand swim theocean is glorious! Received ordersto shift our camp. We are to join inwith the 3rd Coy A.S.C., Major Tedderwill be the C.O: q No further news of mygoing away.

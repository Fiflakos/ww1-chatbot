Author: Frederick Harold Tubb. Date: Monday 07.06.1915. Nationality: Australian. Heard last night thatCapt Finlayson had his wounded legamputated last Saturday, (Extremely sorry!Flies are in myriads & very annoying,Major Tedder at mess last night who hadjust come out from Alexandria said that17 troopships had been sent to theDardanelles for more wounded, AchiBabi had not yet fallen. Evidentlyfierce fighting has been going on latelyOur Bde has not yet gone to Lemnos fras before stated. Received a messagefrom O.room Sgt Wilkinson saying thatCapt Finlayson was much improved -No news yet of my going!
